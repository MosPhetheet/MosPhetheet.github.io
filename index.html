I am Jirapat "Mos" Phetheet, a professional hydrogeologist born and raised in Thailand. With a focus on groundwater exploration and numerical modeling, I am dedicated to applying my expertise to solve complex water resource challenges. I specialize in groundwater flow modeling, data analysis, and GIS applications, aiming to contribute to sustainable water management practices.
